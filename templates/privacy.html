Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly (the "Service") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide you with a personal expense tracking experience. This includes:

  • Account information — such as your name, email address, and password, provided when you register.
  • Expense data — the transactions you log, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.
  • Usage information — basic technical details such as your browser type and the pages you visit, used to keep the Service working reliably.

We do not ask for or store your bank account numbers, card details, or any direct connection to your financial institutions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service, including displaying your expenses and spending summaries.
  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Improve and troubleshoot the Service.
  • Communicate with you about important changes or account-related matters.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your expense information for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored so that it can be retrieved and displayed back to you whenever you use the Service. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. You remain responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential and for maintaining your own backup of any important records.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on a small number of third-party services — for example, hosting providers or fonts loaded from external sources — to operate the Service. These providers may process limited technical data on our behalf and are expected to handle it responsibly.

We do not share your personal expense data with third parties for their own marketing purposes. Any third-party service we use is limited to what is necessary to run Spendly.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
